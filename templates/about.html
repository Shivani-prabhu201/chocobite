Our Story

Born from a love for chocolate, crafted with purpose

Where It All Began

ChocoBite was founded with one simple dream: to make premium, ethical chocolate accessible to everyone in India. What started as a small family kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved artisan chocolate brand.

We source our cacao directly from small-scale farmers in Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and internationally from Ghana and Ecuador. Every bean is selected for quality, and every product is crafted with honesty — no artificial additives, ever.

Our Mission

To bring the world's finest chocolate flavours to your doorstep while supporting sustainable farming and fair trade practices that uplift cocoa-farming communities.

✦ Our Craft ✦

Before the Bean.
Beyond the Bar.

Farming

Nurturing the Cacao

Our farmer-partner community nurtures Indian-origin cacao across Kerala and Tamil Nadu. With sustainable, shade-grown practices and zero synthetic pesticides, every pod is tended with the care it deserves — long before it reaches our hands.

Fermentation

Revealing Hidden Flavours

Controlled fermentation and precise drying unlock the true flavour potential locked inside every cacao bean. Fruity, floral, earthy — each origin tells a different story. We don't mask it; we amplify it.

Chocolate Making

Expressing Cacao's Flavours

Beans are delicately roasted, stone-ground, conched and tempered into chocolate that honours their origin. Every bar is a direct expression of the land, the farmer, and the season — nothing more, nothing less.

Chocolatiering

Exploring Endless Possibilities

Our chocolatiers interpret the couverture into an ever-growing world of bars, bonbons, powders and snacks — balancing familiar Indian tastes with novel flavour combinations. The craft never stops evolving.
